About Our Company

At Nyokiri Global Minerals Ltd, we specialize in global mineral trading, responsible sourcing of precious metals, international logistics, and professional refinery services. Our company is committed to connecting mineral producers, suppliers, and international markets through reliable, transparent, and efficient trade solutions. With a strong understanding of the global mineral industry, we work closely with trusted partners to ensure that all products meet high standards of quality, authenticity, and compliance with international trade regulations. Our services cover the full supply chain of mineral trading, from sourcing valuable precious metals and gemstones to handling transportation, documentation, and refinery coordination. Through our experienced team and strong logistics network, we ensure safe and efficient delivery of mineral products to clients across different parts of the world. Our mission is to deliver high-quality mineral products to global markets while maintaining integrity, sustainability, and professionalism in every transaction. We are dedicated to building long-term partnerships with our clients and stakeholders by providing reliable services, transparent operations, and consistent value in the international mineral trade industry.
